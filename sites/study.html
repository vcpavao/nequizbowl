New England Quiz Bowl

Study Resources

Below is a list of resources that can help teams dramatically improve their performance at tournaments.

  • NAQT Frequency Lists — lists of answers to different topics on NAQT sets are available for purchase on their website.
  • Protobowl — a real-time quiz bowl simulation game.
  • Quizbowl Packet Archive — some past sets, such as Harvard Fall and PACE, are available to download for free.
  • You Gotta Know! — NAQT's lists and brief summaries of recurring topics in quiz bowl questions.
